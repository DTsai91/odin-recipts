Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin pie is an all-time holiday dessert classic, but maybe it's time for a glow up. For Thanksgiving this year, Allrecipes challenged 5 of our favorite bakers to upgrade our basic pumpkin pie recipe.

Ingredients
  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
  • 1 (16 ounce) can pumpkin puree
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 ½ teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees C. Fit pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and place it on a baking sheet.
  2. Melt finely-chopped chocolate
  3. Combine pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, and pumpkin pie spice in a large boewl; mix until combined.
  4. ½ cup of the pumpkin pie filling and stir it into the bowl of melted chocolate until smooth.
  5. Pour pie filling into pie shell. Dollop the chocolate pie filling on top. Use toothpick to create a marbled effect.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 175 degrees C and continue to bake until filling is set, 35 to 40 minutes. Remove from oven, set on a wire rack, and cool completely before serving.
